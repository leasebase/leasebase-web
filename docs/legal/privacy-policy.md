# LeaseBase Privacy Policy (v1)

**Effective Date:** [TBD]  
**Company:** LeaseBase Inc.  
**Website:** leasebase.ai

## 1. Introduction

LeaseBase Inc. ("LeaseBase," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the LeaseBase platform and website located at leasebase.ai.

By using LeaseBase, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from users of the LeaseBase platform.

### Personal Information

This may include:

- name
- email address
- mailing address
- phone number
- account credentials

For property owners and tenants, we may also collect:

- property addresses
- lease documents
- maintenance records
- communication history within the platform

### Payment Information

Rent payments are processed by Stripe, our third-party payment processor.

LeaseBase does not store full payment card numbers or banking credentials.

Stripe may collect and process:

- payment card details
- bank account information
- billing addresses
- transaction history

Your use of payment features is subject to Stripe's privacy policies.

### Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about how the platform is accessed and used.

This may include:

- IP address
- browser type
- device information
- pages visited
- interaction with platform features

## 3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

LeaseBase uses cookies and similar technologies to support platform functionality and analytics.

Cookies may be used for:

- authentication and session management
- remembering user preferences
- analytics and platform performance monitoring

LeaseBase uses Google Analytics to understand how users interact with the platform.

Users may configure their browser settings to refuse cookies; however, some platform functionality may be limited.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

LeaseBase uses collected information to:

- operate and maintain the platform
- process rent payments
- manage user accounts
- deliver product features
- store lease documents and maintenance records
- improve platform performance and usability
- provide customer support
- send service notifications and updates

LeaseBase may also send:

- transactional emails
- product updates
- promotional communications

Users may unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

User data is stored on infrastructure operated by Amazon Web Services (AWS).

LeaseBase currently stores user data in United States data centers located in the California region.

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

However, no internet transmission or electronic storage system is completely secure.

## 6. Data Retention

LeaseBase may retain user information and documents for as long as necessary to:

- operate the platform
- comply with legal obligations
- resolve disputes
- improve service functionality

When users close their accounts, LeaseBase may anonymize or remove personally identifiable information while retaining non-identifiable data for analytics and operational purposes.

## 7. Information Sharing

LeaseBase does not sell user personal information.

We may share information with trusted service providers that help us operate the platform, including:

- Stripe for payment processing
- Amazon Web Services for cloud infrastructure
- Google Analytics for usage analytics
- Google (Gmail) for communication services

These providers process information only to perform services on our behalf.

LeaseBase may also disclose information if required to comply with applicable law, legal processes, or governmental requests.

## 8. Your Privacy Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

These rights may include the ability to:

- request access to personal information we hold about you
- request deletion of personal information
- request correction of inaccurate personal information
- opt out of certain data sharing practices

Requests may be submitted by contacting us using the information provided below.

## 9. Children's Privacy

LeaseBase services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

## 10. International Users

LeaseBase currently operates within the United States.

If the platform expands internationally, additional privacy protections may apply depending on applicable laws.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

LeaseBase may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Users will be notified of material changes through the platform or via email.

Continued use of the platform after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
