# LeaseBase Tenant User Agreement (v1)

**Effective Date:** [TBD]  
**Company:** LeaseBase Inc.  
**Website:** leasebase.ai

This Tenant User Agreement ("Agreement") governs the use of the LeaseBase platform by tenants who receive lease invitations, submit rent payments, or interact with property owners through LeaseBase.

This Agreement supplements the LeaseBase Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, and Payment Terms.

By creating an account or using LeaseBase as a tenant, you agree to this Agreement.

## 1. Eligibility

To use LeaseBase as a tenant, you must:

- be at least 18 years old
- have received an invitation from a property owner or landlord
- provide accurate account information.

LeaseBase may require email verification or identity confirmation for certain platform features.

## 2. Role of LeaseBase

LeaseBase provides software tools that allow tenants and property owners to manage rental relationships digitally.

LeaseBase:

- is not the landlord
- is not a property manager
- does not own or manage rental properties
- is not responsible for property conditions
- is not responsible for lease enforcement

All lease agreements exist solely between the tenant and the property owner.

LeaseBase is not a party to any lease agreement.

## 3. Tenant Accounts

Tenants must create an account to access platform features such as:

- signing lease agreements
- submitting rent payments
- submitting maintenance requests
- communicating with property owners.

Tenants are responsible for maintaining the security of their account credentials.

Tenants are responsible for all activity occurring under their account.

## 4. Lease Agreements

Lease agreements signed through the LeaseBase platform are contracts between the tenant and the property owner.

LeaseBase does not draft, review, or enforce lease agreements.

LeaseBase is not responsible for:

- lease terms
- lease legality
- lease enforcement.

Tenants should review lease agreements carefully and consult legal professionals if necessary.

## 5. Rent Payments

LeaseBase provides tools that allow tenants to submit rent payments electronically.

Payments are processed by Stripe through Stripe Connect Express.

Payment flow:

Tenant → Stripe → Property Owner

LeaseBase does not hold or control funds.

## 6. Payment Methods

Tenants may pay rent using supported payment methods including:

- ACH bank transfers
- debit cards
- credit cards.

Payment processing fees may apply depending on the selected payment method.

These fees may be charged by the payment processor.

## 7. Payment Failures

If a payment fails due to insufficient funds or payment method errors:

- the tenant will be notified
- the property owner may be notified
- the system may attempt to retry the payment.

LeaseBase does not guarantee successful payment processing.

Tenants remain responsible for rent obligations under their lease agreements.

## 8. Maintenance Requests

Tenants may submit maintenance requests through the LeaseBase platform.

Property owners are responsible for reviewing and responding to maintenance requests.

LeaseBase does not:

- perform repairs
- guarantee response times
- ensure maintenance completion.

All maintenance responsibilities remain with the property owner.

## 9. Property Conditions

LeaseBase does not inspect or verify rental property conditions.

LeaseBase is not responsible for:

- property safety
- habitability
- building maintenance
- landlord compliance with housing laws.

Any concerns regarding property conditions must be addressed directly with the property owner.

## 10. Acceptable Use

Tenants agree not to use the platform to:

- submit fraudulent payments
- harass property owners or vendors
- upload illegal or abusive content
- attempt to compromise the security of the platform.

LeaseBase may suspend accounts that violate acceptable use rules.

## 11. Payment Disputes

If a tenant disputes a payment with their financial institution, the dispute will be handled through the payment processor's dispute process.

Tenants acknowledge that LeaseBase does not control payment dispute outcomes.

Refund decisions are made by the property owner.

## 12. Security and Payment Data

LeaseBase does not store full payment card numbers or bank credentials.

Payment information is processed securely by Stripe.

## 13. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, LeaseBase shall not be liable for:

- property conditions
- landlord actions
- maintenance delays
- tenant-landlord disputes
- payment processing failures.

LeaseBase provides the platform "as is."

## 14. Termination of Tenant Accounts

LeaseBase may suspend or terminate tenant accounts for:

- fraud
- abusive behavior
- illegal activity
- misuse of the platform.

Termination of platform access does not affect lease obligations between the tenant and the property owner.

## 15. Changes to this Agreement

LeaseBase may update this Agreement periodically.

Users will be notified of material changes through the platform or via email.

Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
