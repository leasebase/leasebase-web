## 1. Payment Processing Overview

LeaseBase provides software tools that enable tenants to submit rent payments to property owners through the platform.

LeaseBase does not act as a bank, escrow service, or money transmitter.

LeaseBase does not hold, store, or control tenant funds.

All payment processing is performed by a third-party payment processor.

## 2. Third-Party Payment Processor

Payments on the LeaseBase platform are processed by Stripe through the Stripe Connect Express platform.

Property owners must create and maintain a connected Stripe account to receive rent payments.

By using LeaseBase payment features, users agree to comply with Stripe's applicable terms, policies, and requirements.

LeaseBase is not responsible for Stripe's services or payment processing systems.

## 3. Payment Flow

Rent payments follow this processing flow:

Tenant → Stripe → Property Owner

Payments are transferred directly from the tenant's payment method to the property owner's connected Stripe account.

LeaseBase does not take custody of funds at any time.

## 4. Supported Payment Methods

Tenants may pay rent through supported payment methods including:

- ACH bank transfers
- debit cards
- credit cards

Payment availability may vary based on Stripe capabilities and geographic location.
