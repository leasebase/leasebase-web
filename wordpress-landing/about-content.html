Our Story

LeaseBase was born from frustration. Our founder, a property owner managing 40+ rental units, was juggling spreadsheets, multiple software tools, and endless manual tasks. There had to be a better way.

After spending months testing expensive property management software that was either too complex or too basic, he decided to build the solution himself. The goal was simple: create a platform that combines the power of enterprise software with the simplicity independent landlords need.

Today, LeaseBase serves over 2,000 property owners managing 15,000+ units across the country. We're proud to be the platform that helps independent landlords compete, grow, and succeed.
