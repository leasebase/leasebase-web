Frequently asked questions

How does LeaseBase pricing work?

LeaseBase offers a free Starter plan for basic property management, and a Growth plan at $9 per active unit per month for owners who want automation, e-signatures, autopay, and enhanced workflows. You only pay for units with active leases.

Is pricing based on properties or units?

Pricing is based on active units, not properties. If you have a duplex with two rented units, you pay for two units. Vacant units with no active lease are not charged.

Can I start free?

Yes. The Starter plan is completely free and includes property management, tenant onboarding, basic lease creation, rent collection, and maintenance requests. No credit card required.

What do tenants get access to?

Tenants are invited by the property owner and get their own dashboard where they can pay rent online, submit and track maintenance requests, and access their lease and account information. Tenants do not sign up independently — access is always through the owner's invitation. This is included in all plans.

What happens when I grow?

LeaseBase scales with you. Start free with Starter, upgrade to Growth when you need more automation, and contact us about Portfolio if you need multi-user access, advanced reporting, or support for larger operations.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

No. LeaseBase is billed monthly with no long-term contracts. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.

Can I contact LeaseBase for larger portfolios?

Absolutely. If you manage a larger portfolio or need advanced capabilities like multi-user access and custom reporting, reach out via our contact form and we'll work with you on the right plan.
