Frequently Asked Questions

❓

Can I switch plans later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

❓

What are the payment processing fees?

Starter: 2.9% + $0.30, Professional: 1.9% + $0.30, Enterprise: 1.5% + $0.30 per transaction.

❓

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees. Ever. We believe in transparent, straightforward pricing.

❓

What happens if I exceed my unit limit?

We'll notify you and help you upgrade to the appropriate plan. Your service won't be interrupted.

❓

Do you offer custom enterprise plans?

Yes! For portfolios over 200 units or special requirements, contact our sales team for custom pricing.
